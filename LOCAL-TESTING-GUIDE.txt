================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Problem: Can't access any login page
------------------------------------
Solution:
1. Edit functions.php line 163
2. Comment it: // require_once SHIVENDRA_THEME_DIR . '/inc/custom-login.php';
3. Access: http://lifevelo.com/wp-admin


Problem: White screen / Error
-----------------------------
Check: /wp-content/debug.log
Look for PHP errors in custom-login.php


Problem: 404 error on custom URL
--------------------------------
Solution:
1. Login to wp-admin
2. Go to Settings > Permalinks
3. Click "Save Changes" (flush rewrite rules)
4. Try custom URL again
